ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Kristi Pannia, the founder and owner of Kristi's Cleaning Services. With over 35 years of experience, cleaning is my true passion, and I've had the pleasure of serving over 500 happy clients by turning houses into truly clean and comfortable homes.

Your peace of mind is our priority. That's why we're fully bonded and insured, guaranteeing that every sweep and scrub is performed with the highest level of professionalism and accountability. When we step into your home, you're covered.

When my team and I work for you, we promise three things:

  • Thoroughness: I don't believe in shortcuts. We meticulously care for every corner, ensuring a truly spotless result.
  • Honesty & Respect: We treat your home and privacy with the highest respect. We are always responsible, reliable, and accountable for our work.
  • Personalized Care: We know every home is unique. Tell us about your preferences! We happily tailor our cleaning methods and can use natural, allergy-friendly products upon request to ensure your family's health is never compromised.

We are proud to serve our local communities, including Brampton, Bolton, Caledon, Orangeville, Woodbridge and Vaughan. We look forward to making your life simpler and your home sparkle!
